About Us

About Us & Our Offer

Our company, Aurora is an online library platform which addresses the e-books in general. We also provide the access to the many categories such as books, newspaper, comics, audiobooks and etc.
We offer a membership program after some free trials where users pay to read the books online. We not only recommend new books and show the trending of the books but also lead them to local libraries if the users wish to access actual books.

Potential Customers

  • People who cannot visit offline library due to COVID 19
  • Busy, modern people who tend to read books via online
  • Students who prefer using devices to read books for assignments or for their own goods
  • People who wish to read online due to the cost of buying actual books or to save the paper

Why you should try our service

COVID 19 has increased the number of people who avoid using public facilities and put the restrictions on the use of them. This website provides services for the users to read books they need safely and quickly at home, and for users who cannot go to libraries, it recommends nearby bookstores based on their location.
